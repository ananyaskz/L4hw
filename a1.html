Ramen

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle soup dish made with wheat noodles in a meat or fish-based broth.It is known for its rich and complex flavors, often enhanced with ingredients like soy sauce, miso paste, or pork.Ramen can be served hot or cold, and is often garnished with ingredients like sliced pork, green onions, or boiled eggs.
